About Me

Hello there! I'm Amir Banks, an aspiring coder based in Seattle, Washington. With a passion for technology and a drive to make a positive impact,

I've dedicated my career to improving the performance of organizations that serve thousands of individuals each year.

My journey in the professional world has equipped me with a diverse skill set and a wealth of experience. From my time as a Lab Technician at Fastest Labs in Tacoma to my role as a Telecommunications Tech at AT&T in Puyallup, I've honed my abilities in website development, information security, and technical support.

But my story doesn't stop there. As a Sales Associate at H&M in Olympia and Assistant Manager at Ausum Vapor in Lacey, I've cultivated leadership skills and a keen eye for promoting high-quality services and programs.

My educational background includes training in facilities maintenance from Columbia Basin Job Corps, where I completed coursework in various trades. Additionally, I hold a High School Diploma from Bartlett High in Anchorage, Alaska.

Proficient in a range of software and tools, including MS Word, WordPress, Salesforce, and more, I'm always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies. With a career history of training and supervising staff, promoting adherence to organizational policies, and leading projects to success, I'm ready to take on new challenges and make a meaningful contribution to the world of coding and digital innovation.

Feel free to reach out to me at abanks@sea.yearup.org. Let's connect and explore how we can work together to drive positive change through technology!
